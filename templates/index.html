PCOS(Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) :

  • Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is the most common hormonal disorder in women of reproductive age.
  • The global prevalence of PCOS is estimated between 4% and 20%. The World Health Organization (WHO) data suggests that approximately 116 million women (3.4%) are affected by PCOS globally.
